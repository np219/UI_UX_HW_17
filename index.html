Hi there!

I’m a UIUX Designer in the DC Metro area. I’m passionate about creating user experiences through visual and interactive design that are impactful and meaningful.

What initially piqued my interest in UIUX Design are the creative and visual aspect of the discipline. The careful and thoughtful act of creating something from nothing has always been rewarding for me. I enjoy the cathartic process of obsessing over all the details and changing and adapting until I have something that I am proud of.

What further inspired me about design is realizing its power to influence and bring joy to others. That was when I fully understood the principle of designing for the human experience. If all these small but significant decisions can drive positive change in our lives, then I want to be a part of that journey.

My UX skills

USER RESEARCH // I follow the data. I conduct interviews, usability and A/B Testing to understand the problem(s) and gain user insights. Other research tools used include surveys and web analytics.

USER EMPATHY // To understand the user, my go-to is to observe and listen. Other tools include affinity diagrams, persona creation, storyboarding, and journey mapping

QUALITATIVE INTERVIEWS // I avoid asking closed-ended questions and instead ask general questions that elicit the participant tell me more. For example: “How do you make a sandwich?”
